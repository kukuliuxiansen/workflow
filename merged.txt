### Episode 1

**Scene 1-1**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Mystic High, Headmaster's Office**
**Characters: Headmaster Blackwood, Nick**

△ Moonlight streams through a Gothic window, illuminating the Headmaster's office filled with ancient tomes. Headmaster Blackwood, a wizard with piercing eyes, stands by the window.

Blackwood: (Turns, voice warm) Welcome to Mystic High. Your test results are quite intriguing. Your energy signature doesn't match any known supernatural entity.

Nick: (Voice low) I don't know what I am. Just that I'm not like everyone else.

Blackwood: (Smiles slightly) We'll help you figure that out.

△ The office door opens. Ariana Thompson enters. She has long curly brown hair, striking amber eyes, and wears the crisp school uniform with an air of confidence.

Blackwood: (Gestures) Captain Thompson, this is our new student. Please show him around.

Ariana: (Eyes narrowing slightly as she assesses Nick, then smiles warmly) Welcome. I'm Ariana. Let's get started.

**Scene 1-2**

**Night/Exterior/Interior**
**Location: Campus Corridors / Nick's Dorm**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana**

△ Ariana leads Nick through winding corridors, past a vast library and a courtyard where students gather around a bonfire. Nick is quiet, observant.

Ariana: (Stops at a dorm room) This is your room. You'll have a roommate, but he's not here. Make yourself at home.

△ Nick nods. Ariana leaves. As Nick is about to close the door, a group of boys pushes it open.

**Scene 1-3**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Nick's Dorm**
**Characters: Nick, Jack, Jack's Pack**

△ The leader, a tall, muscular boy with messy blond hair, sneers. It's Jack.

Jack: (Sneering) A newbie.

△ Nick extends a hand, trying to be friendly.

Nick: I'm Nick. Nick Ross.

△ The boys laugh, leaving his hand hanging. Jack pushes Nick hard.

Jack: (Growls) On your knees, newbie. Tell us what you are. What power do you have?

△ Nick hesitates, fear and frustration rising. Jack's eyes begin to glow a fierce red. His body contorts, stretching, turning into a werewolf. He pounces, claws swiping the air. Nick tries to dodge but is too slow. Jack scratches his arm, leaving a deep, bloody gash. Nick screams, the sound drowned out by the pack's cheers. No one comes to help. He is alone at their mercy.

### Episode 2

**Scene 2-1**

**Morning/Interior**
**Location: Nick's Dorm / Bathroom**
**Characters: Nick**

△ Sunlight streams into the room. Nick groans, rolls over, and looks at the clock: 8:30 AM.

Nick: (Jumps out of bed) Oh my God!

△ He rushes into the shower. Under the warm water, he notices a strange thing—no pain from last night's injuries. He checks the mirror. The gash is gone, his skin smooth and unblemished.

Nick: (Stares, awed) Bloody hell! Was that a terrible nightmare?

△ As he stands there, lost in thought, a flicker of energy like lightning flashes in his eyes. He shakes his head, dismisses it, and continues his shower.

**Scene 2-2**

**Morning/Interior**
**Location: Classroom**
**Characters: Nick, Jack, Professor Everwood, Students**

△ Nick walks into the bustling classroom, relieved he hasn't missed much. He heads for a seat. Jack rises from his chair, eyes fixed on him with hatred, blocking his path.

Jack: (Low, menacing) Look who's here. I thought you wouldn't make it out of bed.

Nick: (Trying to stay calm) Stay away from me. Let me be.

△ Jack grabs Nick's shoulder and flings him across the classroom. Nick crashes into a desk. The room falls silent. Nick struggles to his feet, eyes blazing.

Jack: (Sneers) No one walks out on me like that! No one!

△ The classroom door bursts open. Professor Everwood, tall and imposing with piercing green eyes, walks in, surveying the scene.

Everwood: (Firm) What's going on here?

Jack: (Voice dripping with sarcasm) Just a little disagreement, Professor.

Everwood: (Eyes narrowing) You're not here to fight, Jack. You're here to learn. Behave. (Turns to Nick) Are you okay?

Nick: (Rubbing his shoulder) I'm fine, Professor.

**Scene 2-3**

**Day/Exterior**
**Location: Hallway outside Classroom**
**Characters: Nick, Jack**

△ Class ends. Jack catches up to Nick in the hallway.

Jack: (Sneers) You think you're so smart? You're just a weak newbie who doesn't know anything about being a supernatural.

Nick: (Eyes flashing) I may not know everything. But I know one thing. I'm not afraid of you, Jack.

△ Jack's face twists into a snarl, but he says nothing. He turns and walks away, leaving Nick wondering what he's gotten himself into.

### Episode 3

**Scene 3-1**

**Day/Exterior**
**Location: Hallway**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana**

△ Someone taps Nick from behind. He startles, then feels relief seeing Ariana.

Ariana: (Concerned) Are you okay?

Nick: (Trying to sound nonchalant) I'll be fine.

Ariana: (Not convinced) You don't look fine. Is it true Jack attacked you last night?

Nick: (Nods) It happened.

Ariana: (Scanning him) How come there's no sign of it?

Nick: (Confused) I don't know. I woke up this morning, and I was fine.

Ariana: (Eyes narrowing, mind racing) I'm yet to figure that out too. For now, I'm just a normal guy.

Ariana: (Smiles softly) No, you're not. None of us are.

△ They share a gaze, a strange connection forming.

**Scene 3-2**

**Day/Exterior**
**Location: Hallway near lockers**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Jack**

△ Heavy footsteps. Jack appears, eyes blazing with anger at seeing them together.

Jack: (Sneers) You think you can just waltz in and steal her from me? She's mine!

Ariana: (Countenance hardens) What are you talking about, Jack?

△ Jack laughs coldly, grabs Nick, and smashes his head against a metal locker with a loud clang. Nick stumbles, vision blurring.

Jack: (Bellowing) Stay away from her! She's mine!

Ariana: (Voice trembling with rage) What did you just do?

Jack: (Sneers) He's just unconscious. You belong to me and—

Ariana: (Lashes out) To hell with that! I belong to no one!

△ A crowd of students gathers. Professor Everwood appears.

Everwood: (Demanding) What's happening here? (Sees Nick) Jack, what have you done now? Headmaster's office. Now.

**Scene 3-3**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Magical Healing Clinic**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Elara (Healer Witch)**

△ Two students carry the unconscious Nick into the cozy but magically-stocked clinic. Ariana follows. A witch with long, flowing white hair emerges—Elara.

Elara: (Warm voice) Hello, Ariana. A new patient? What seems to be the matter?

△ Ariana hesitates, then pours out the whole story, including Nick's mysterious healing. Elara listens intently, then begins preparing healing spells and potions. Ariana sits by Nick, holding his hand, watching Elara work, unable to shake the feeling that something is deeply off about Nick.

### Episode 4

**Scene 4-1**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Magical Healing Clinic**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Elara**

△ Nick lies unconscious. The bleeding from his head has stopped; the wound has sealed over with new flesh.

Elara: (Eyes wide with wonder) This is incredible. My healing spells didn't do this. He's healing himself, at an incredible rate.

Ariana: (Whispers) What is he?

Elara: (Shakes head) I don't know, but I intend to find out.

△ Suddenly, Nick's body begins to glow with a soft, then intensely bright, blue light. Ariana and Elara shield their eyes. When they open them, Nick's body is enveloped in flashes of crackling lightning.

Ariana: (Exclaims) What the hell?

Elara: (Pale with concern) I don't know what's happening.

△ The lightning intensifies, as if rewriting him at a molecular level, then abruptly stops. Nick remains unconscious.

**Scene 4-2**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Headmaster's Office**
**Characters: Headmaster Blackwood, Professor Everwood, Jack**

△ Jack stands before the massive ornate desk, pale and tense. Everwood recounts the events. Blackwood's face grows stern.

Blackwood: (Thunders) The purpose of this school is to teach you control! Not to indulge in senseless violence! (Stands, moves closer to Jack) This isn't the first time you've hurt a student. You will be punished. I will speak to your parents. If they do nothing, you will be expelled!

△ Jack stands frozen, staring at the floor, as Blackwood's rage fills the room.

**Scene 4-3**

**Dream Sequence/Night**
**Location: Barren, Desolate Landscape**
**Characters: Nick, Azazel**

△ Nick's soul finds itself in a desolate, oppressive darkness.

Nick: (Calls out) Hello! Is anyone there?

A Voice: (Echoes) I have been waiting for you!

△ The darkness recedes, replaced by brilliant light. A tall, imposing man in ancient bronze warrior armor emerges. A sword is strapped to his side, a golden helmet on his head. He radiates power.

Nick: (Barely a whisper) Who are you?

The Warrior: (Eyes flash like lightning) I am Azazel. I am your ancestor. I have been waiting for you.

△ As he speaks, wings sprout from his back, beating the air. Nick feels a strange tingling, and suddenly wings sprout from his own back. He is dressed in similar armor, holding a flaming sword.

Nick: (Exclaims, excited) Oh my gosh! I am like you!

Azazel: (Nods, smiling slightly) Yes. You are my descendant. You possess the same powers.

Nick: (Curious) What does that make us?

△ Before Azazel can answer, Nick is pulled back to reality. He opens his eyes in the clinic to find Ariana beside him.

Ariana: (Smiles) Welcome back. I was worried.

△ Nick sits up, disoriented, his mind reeling from the encounter.

Nick: (Fascinated) I'm still trying to learn about myself.

Ariana: (Eyes sparkling) I'm sure you'll figure it out.

△ Ariana tells him about the lightning that enveloped him in the clinic. Nick listens, fascinated, but keeps his encounter with Azazel a secret.

**Scene 5-2**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: School Canteen**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Jack**

△ Jack sits nearby, using his enhanced hearing to eavesdrop. Jealousy burns as he watches them laugh together. He storms over, grabs Nick from behind, and tosses him away like a ragdoll.

Ariana: (Lashes out) What the hell! Doing this will never impress any girl here!

Jack: (Sneers, face twisted) We'll see about that.

△ He turns towards Nick, who is struggling to his feet, and punches him, sending him flying across the hall. Ariana tries to intervene but is pushed aside.

**Scene 5-3**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: School Canteen**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Jack, Students**

△ Jealousy consumes Jack as he heads toward Nick, intent on finishing him. But before he can reach him, Nick springs to his feet, eyes surging with lightning-like energy. The air crackles. A brilliant light envelops him. When it fades, he stands in magnificent armor, a flaming sword in his grasp, magnificent golden wings sprouting from his back.

Students: (Gasp in amazement) A god!

△ Nick beats his wings, creating a whirlwind. Ariana holds onto a bolted-down table. Jack is sent flying across the hall.

Ariana: (Exclaims) Oh, dude! You are a god!

△ As the chaos dies, Nick stands tall, wings beating slowly. His eyes meet Ariana's, and he smiles. The canteen is silent, students stunned.

### Episode 6

**Scene 6-1**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: School Canteen**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Jack, Professor Everwood, Headmaster Blackwood, Students**

△ The canteen is in chaos. Nick hovers in the air, golden wings beating slowly, flaming sword crackling in his hand. Students scream and scatter, hiding behind overturned tables. Jack's pack retreats to the far corner.

△ Ariana gets up from behind a table and runs towards Nick. Professor Everwood steps in front of her, blocking her path.

Everwood: (To Nick, voice calm but commanding) Release it. Now.

Nick: (Confused, voice strained) I don't know how.

△ Headmaster Blackwood strides forward and strikes his staff against the floor. The sound cracks through the room. A pulse of energy rolls outward, hitting Nick like cold water. His wings jerk violently, then fold in on themselves, dissolving into sparks of gold light. His armor fades piece by piece. The sword vanishes with a hiss. Nick's legs give out. Professor Everwood catches him before he hits the floor.

Everwood: (Through clenched teeth) I've got him.

△ Ariana is at his side instantly, her fingers wrapping around his wrist.

Ariana: (Whispers) I'm here. I'm right here.

Blackwood: (Orders) Clinic. Now.

**Scene 6-2**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Magical Healing Clinic**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Elara**

△ Nick drifts back to consciousness. The clinic smells of herbs, burnt sage, and something sweet and bitter. His body feels heavy. Ariana sits beside him on a stool, looking exhausted, a smear of soot on her cheek. Elara, the healer witch, steps into view.

Nick: (Crops) Did I hurt anyone?

Ariana: (Quickly) No. You scared everyone half to death, but no one's hurt.

Nick: (Closes eyes, relief washing over him) I didn't want to... I couldn't stop it.

Elara: (Places fingers gently on Nick's temple) You drained yourself. Whatever you released was far more than your body could safely hold.

**Scene 6-3**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Magical Healing Clinic**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Elara, Headmaster Blackwood, Professor Everwood**

△ The clinic door opens. Headmaster Blackwood and Professor Everwood enter, their expressions grave.

Blackwood: (Quietly) Leave us.

△ Elara hesitates, then nods and leaves with Everwood. Blackwood moves closer to the bed.

Blackwood: You collapsed. Do you know why?

Nick: (Staring at the ceiling) I was empty. Like something scooped me out.

Blackwood: That is accurate. Power without control always demands payment. (Pauses) You said there was more you wished to explain.

Nick: (Pushes himself up slowly) When I was out before... not in the canteen. After Jack attacked me... I didn't just black out. I was somewhere else. Empty. Dark. And someone was there.

Blackwood: (Eyes sharp) Who?

Nick: He said his name was Azazel. He said I was his descendant. That I carried the same power.

△ The temperature in the room seems to drop. Blackwood's expression doesn't change, but something dark flickers in his eyes.

Blackwood: (Turns and calls out) Elara.

△ Elara enters immediately. Blackwood speaks a single word: Azazel. Elara's face tightens.

Elara: Then it's confirmed.

Nick: (Looking between them) Confirmed what?

Elara: That your bloodline is far older—and far more dangerous—than we hoped.

Blackwood: (Faces Nick) You are a Nephilim. The child of a fallen angel and a human.

### Episode 8

**Scene 8-1**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Holding Cell Corridor**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Professor Everwood, Elara**

△ Nick, Ariana, Everwood, and Elara walk down a cold, damp corridor towards a reinforced iron door. Sigils on the door hum as Nick approaches.

Everwood: (Stops) You don't step inside unless I say so.

Nick: (Throat tight) He's in there.

Everwood: Yes. And whatever is wearing him is contained.

△ Everwood taps the door with her staff. The lock slides open on its own. They all flinch.

**Scene 8-2**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Holding Cell**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Professor Everwood, Elara, Jack**

△ The door opens. Jack stands calmly in the center of the room, hands folded behind his back, posture relaxed. He looks too normal.

Jack: (Calmly) Nick.

△ Nick's stomach twists. The pull under his ribs flares.

Ariana: (Steps forward, blocking Nick) You don't get to say his name like that.

Jack: (Eyes slide to her) You're still guarding him. Cute.

Everwood: (Snaps) Enough.

△ Jack's gaze returns to Nick, studying him.

Jack: (Faint smile) You feel it, don't you? The pull.

Nick: (Steps forward) I feel you.

Jack: Good.

Everwood: Answer the question. What happened to you?

Jack: (Shrugs, careless) I woke up.

Nick: Woke up where?

Jack: Where I was invited.

**Scene 8-3**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Holding Cell**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Professor Everwood, Elara, Jack**

Jack: (Eyes sharp) You already know. The night you manifested fully, the school's older lines woke. Not because you wanted them to. Because something in your blood resonated with what's sealed beneath us.

Nick: (Stomach drops) You're saying this is my fault.

Jack: (Shrugs) Fault is a human word.

Nick: What did it say to you?

Jack: (Hesitates) It said I wasn't broken. Just unfinished. That I was strong enough to survive what was coming. That I could help.

Ariana: (Sharp) Help what?

Jack: (Eyes slide back to Nick) Help you.

△ The pull under Nick's ribs tightens painfully. The sigils on the wall brighten.

Everwood: (Steps forward) That's enough.

△ Jack's smile fades. He looks at Nick seriously.

Jack: You opened the door.

Nick: (Heart slams) No.

Jack: Did you think doors only open outward? You answered. You were called. And something in you responded.

△ Nick's chest burns. The pull intensifies. Ariana grabs him.

Ariana: Nick!

Everwood: (Cold) You will be isolated.

Jack: (Shrugs) You can lock me up.

Nick: (Forces himself upright) Why come back at all?

Jack: (Quietly) Because it told me to. That you wouldn't listen unless I said it.

Nick: (Pulse spikes) Said what?

Jack: (Leans forward, voice drops) That the door isn't finished opening. The one that knows your name. You opened it once. And it's learning how to open you back.

**Scene 9-2**

**Night/Interior**
**Location: Headmaster's Office**
**Characters: Headmaster Blackwood, Professor Everwood, Elara, Nick, Ariana**

Everwood: (To Blackwood) We can still call the Council.

Blackwood: (Jaw clenches) And invite containment.

### Episode 10

**Scene 10-1**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Training Hall**
**Characters: Nick, Ariana, Professor Everwood**

△ The training hall smells of stone dust and old sweat. Faded circles and lines mark the floor. Nick stands at the edge, Ariana beside him.

Everwood: (Steps forward) Captain Thompson. Out.

Ariana: (Eyes narrow) No.

Everwood: This training is for him. Not you.

Ariana: He needs support.

Everwood: Support will not stop something that can move through walls. If you want to help, you help by not distracting him.

Ariana: (Looks at Nick) You want me to leave?

Nick: (Hesitates, then nods) Just... wait outside.

Ariana: (Jaw tight) I'll be right outside the door.

**Scene 10-2**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Training Hall**
**Characters: Nick, Professor Everwood**

△ Ariana leaves. The hall feels larger. Everwood tosses Nick a padded staff.

Everwood: This is not a class. This is survival.

Nick: (Swallows) Why now?

Everwood: Because you don't have time to learn slowly.

△ Everwood steps into the center circle on the floor. Nick follows. Everwood's voice is calm and cold.

Everwood: Your power is reactive. It flares when you're afraid or angry.

Nick: I'm working on that.

Everwood: Working is not enough.

△ Everwood steps forward, staff tapping. A faint shimmer runs along the circle lines. She tells him to hold his staff properly, then attacks without warning, disarming him instantly.

Everwood: Dead.

Nick: (Heart pounds) You didn't even give me a chance.

Everwood: The thing in the walls will not give you a chance.

**Scene 10-3**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Training Hall**
**Characters: Nick, Professor Everwood**

△ Nick's anger rises, heat surging under his skin. Everwood notices.

Everwood: There it is. Your body argues before your mouth does. Learn to make it obey.

Nick: (Through gritted teeth) Then show me.

△ They spar again and again. Nick gets knocked down repeatedly, his anger fueling clumsy mistakes. Everwood is relentless.

Everwood: You feel small. You feel judged. You feel like everyone sees you as a threat. That feeling is dangerous. It makes you either explode or shut down. You are not allowed to do either.

Nick: (Panting) So what am I allowed to do?

Everwood: (Steps closer) You breathe. You see. You decide.

△ They spar again. This time, Nick blocks. He holds his ground, focusing on control.

Everwood: (Eyes narrow) Better. Not dead.

△ The training continues. Then, suddenly, the air shifts. The pull under Nick's ribs tightens, turning cold.

Nick: (Freezes) It's... moving.

Everwood: (Instantly alert) Where?

Nick: (Eyes drift to the far wall) Near.

Everwood: (Voice sharp) The shadow?

Nick: (Shakes head slowly) No. Something else. The second presence. The one behind the iron door. It heard us.

Everwood: Heard what?

Nick: (Swallows, taste of metal) It heard me learning to fight. And it doesn't like it.

△ The lantern nearest the far wall flickers and dies. Darkness pools in that corner. A slow scraping sound comes from the stone.

Everwood: (Lifts staff) Who's there?

A whisper: (From the darkness, low and pleased) Good... Learn faster.

### Episode 11

**Scene 11-1**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Training Hall**
**Characters: Nick, Professor Everwood**

△ The training hall is quiet. Nick stands in the center circle, breathing hard from the session. The memory of the cold presence lingers.

Everwood: (Studies him) You felt something. In the wall.

Nick: (Nods) It's still there. Watching.

Everwood: It's testing the boundaries. Learning our weaknesses.

Nick: (Looks at his hands) And I'm the one it's watching most.

Everwood: (Firmly) Then you need to be ready. Again.

△ They resume training. Nick pushes himself, focusing on control. But his mind keeps drifting to that cold, watching presence.

**Scene 11-2**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Training Hall**
**Characters: Nick, Professor Everwood**

△ Nick manages to block several strikes, his movements becoming more deliberate. Sweat drips down his face.

Everwood: (A hint of approval) Better. You're thinking instead of reacting.

Everwood: That's instinct. Trust it. But don't let it override your mind.

Everwood: (Steps back, nods once) Not dead. Progress.

Nick: (A small smile) It worked.

Everwood: Barely. Again.

**Scene 11-3**

**Day/Interior**
**Location: Training Hall**
**Characters: Nick, Professor Everwood**

△ As they prepare to resume, the air in the hall shifts. The temperature drops slightly. The lanterns flicker.

Nick: (Freezes) It's back.

Everwood: (Lifts her staff, eyes scanning) Where?

Nick: (Voice low) Everywhere. It's in the walls.
